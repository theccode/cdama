Programs


The mission of C-DAMAA is to increase access to individual, household and firm level data as well as macroeconomic data to promote a culture of interrogating complex relationships among issues using acquired analytical skills. Further to this, C-DAMAA engages industry, government and development partners on research outcomes through the active and regular release of policy briefs and dialogues with all stakeholders.
